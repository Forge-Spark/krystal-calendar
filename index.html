Campaign post (Feel · Believe · Know)

Four months. Four personas. One repeatable content engine. Each month produces the same unit: a persona-targeted campaign landing page, three campaign social posts (Feel, Believe and Know) with paid amplification, and five organic posts covering all four story pillars. The campaigns move a specific audience from awareness to a reason to act. The organic posts build brand trust, reach and distinctiveness in between. Same shape every month. Different persona. Different pillar lead.
