Campaign post (Feel · Believe · Know)

Each month produces the same unit: one persona-targeted campaign landing page, three campaign social posts (Feel, Believe and Know) with paid amplification, and five organic posts covering all four story pillars plus one flexible extension tied to the monthly theme. Instead of asking what to post this week, the plan answers a single question — what is worth saying this month — and everything else ladders up to that. Same shape every month. Different persona. Different pillar lead.
